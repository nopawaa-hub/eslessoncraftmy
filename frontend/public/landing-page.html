Features

One workspace for your entire teaching workflow.

From the first lesson idea to the final parent report — every tool is built around KSSR standards and the realities of a Malaysian classroom.

Lesson Planner AI

Generate full KSSR English RPH — objectives, success criteria, staged procedure, differentiation and assessment — aligned to Content and Learning Standards.

Evaluation Engine

Paste or upload a lesson plan and AI highlights weak pedagogy, teacher-centred habits and low HOTS — with specific comments and remedies, just like a mentor reviewer.

PBD & Assessment

Key in TP bands, record observation evidence and track each pupil against performance criteria — built for classroom-based Pentaksiran Bilik Darjah.

Timetable

Visualise your weekly English schedule, link lessons to classes and spot gaps so every period is prepared and no RPH slips through.

Materials Library

Keep worksheets, audio, picture cards and model texts organised by skill and topic — ready to attach to any lesson in seconds.

Analytics

See mastery distribution, skill radar, risk prediction and topic heatmaps — turning raw PBD data into clear intervention decisions.

From sign-in to sent report in four steps.
