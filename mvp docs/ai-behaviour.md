AI Scope for MVP

AI usage must remain lightweight.

AI should ONLY assist with:

quick lesson plan generation
lesson suggestions
simple reminders
lesson summary suggestions

Do NOT generate (it will be implemented onto the later phases):

predictive analytics
complex intervention systems
enterprise automation
AI Lesson Suggestions

suggest classroom activity
suggest teaching material
suggest quick reflection summary
AI UX Principles

assistive
optional
